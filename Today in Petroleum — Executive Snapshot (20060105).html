What You Should Know

  • Prices are pulling back, not breaking: Consolidation follows a strong rally. Risk sentiment, not inventories, is driving the needle.
  • The "India Squeeze": India's pivot to U.S. and Venezuelan crude is forcing Russian Urals into "desperation discounts" (~$25/bbl netbacks).
  • Capital discipline: Majors are protecting cash flow over output, reducing global supply elasticity.
